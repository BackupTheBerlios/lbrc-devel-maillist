Hey,

please check, whether the service announcement makes it into the system
sdp server. The sdpserver is responsible for the publishing of the
services provided by the bluetooth adapter. You can get the list like
this:

mblaesing at enterprise:~$ sdptool browse local

There should be an entry like this:

Service Name: LBRC
Service RecHandle: 0x10004
Protocol Descriptor List:
 "L2CAP" (0x0100)
 "RFCOMM" (0x0003)
   Channel: 1

If it is not listed, something is fishy in the bluez stack (on the linux
side), if it is listed we have to take a look at the phone.
