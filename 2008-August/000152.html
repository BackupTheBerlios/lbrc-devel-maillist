Am Montag, den 18.08.2008, 21:40 +0200 schrieb Matthias Bläsing:
> > - The ganyremote J2ME program knows how to save previously used
> > connections. So scanning for devices becomes an option, you can pick
> > your system from a list. That would be nice for lbrc too.

Ok - to do exaktly this, you would have to introduce a record store.
Instead I decided to stick with what the bluetooth stack offers us
already. You can query the discovery agent for a list of preknown
devices (on my phone this list is the same as under "Meine Geräte" (My
devices"). I asume that on most phones this will be a list of paired
devices. As per Default LBRC requires the phone and the device to be
paired, after the first connection the notebook should be part of the
list.

If you want to test ist, have a look at the svn code and build yourself
or grab your copy from http://www.doppel-helix.eu/LBRC.ja[dr]
