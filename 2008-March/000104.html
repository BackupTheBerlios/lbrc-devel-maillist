It would be interesting to know whether there is a way to make X
generate a symbol. But I asume that on the layer I could see a chance to
inject keystrokes (XTest) keycodes are expected - But I could be wrong

cons. I really would like to see a X-level input system, as this would
eliminate the pain of making the uinput-dbus bridge run.

But I won't have enough time for this in the near future (maybe in the
summer, but I'm really not sure). If someone wants to write such a
module - don't hesitate. I'll look over it, you can ask if you are faced
with problems and you have the source of the uinput module and the other
key listeners.
